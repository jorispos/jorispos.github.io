Introducing the world's first real hoverboard

It is finally here and it is even better than predicted in the movies. Our hoverboard is super easy to control, all you have to do is lean in the direction in which you want to go. The board is equiped with high tech technology to ensure the safety of all users.

How it works

At hover we have discovered a way to harness the energy produced naturally by our very own earth. Our revolutionary magnet found at the bottom of every board is able to repell this energy allowing it to hover above the ground. Thanks to the micro-chips found on board, the hoverboard is always safe to use.

In order to ride the board, the user simply has to lean in the direction in which they intend to go. The board can be turned off and on using voice control causing it to slowly descend/ascend. While hovering over the surface the board levitates 10-20cm above the ground, this can differ based on the weight of the user.
